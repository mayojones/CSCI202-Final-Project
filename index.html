How we got our name

We have always been plant lovers and have had great luck with growing plants. But through my years of experience growing plants and after talking to friend about their plant journeys I thought I would make a place for the people that struggle with plants. I think plants should and can be for anyone, so hearing that people are too scared to get a plant cause they might kill it makes me sad. This is how we got the name Numb Thumb Botany, which basically means the opposite of having a green thumb. There is a plant out ther perfectly ready for you the care for it, you just need to find it.
